WELCOME TO RAJKIYE ENGINEERING COLLEGE GONDA

Rajkiya Engineering College (R.E.C.), Gonda, is established by the Government of Uttar Pradesh in the Year 2023. The college is offering B. Tech Programme in four disciplines Computer Science Engineering (CSE), Electrical Engineering (EE), Civil Engineering (CE) and Mechanical Engineering(ME) with the intake of 60 seats in each branch from the session 2023-24.

The college is running on the Institute of Rajkiye Engineering College Ambedkar Nagar campus for this session only. The institute is a Associated College of Dr. A.P.J. Abdul Kalam Technical University (AKTU), Lucknow. Dr. Govind Pandey has joined as the founder director of this Institute.
